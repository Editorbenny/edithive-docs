For beta editors

The beta guide.

Five minutes from invite to first selects. Here is everything you need, in the order you need it.

Set up in five steps

Install the appYour invite email links to the build for your Mac. Double click the .pkg. It is signed and notarized by Apple, so there is no security warning.
ActivateFirst launch asks for the activation key from the same invite email. Paste it once. Setup finishes in seconds, fully offline.
Install your panelGrab the Premiere Pro panel or the Resolve Studio panel, each a one click installer. In Premiere it appears under Window › Extensions › Edithive on next launch.
Point it at footageOpen Select, choose a folder, an XML, or your open Resolve timeline, and hit Analyze Footage. Everything runs on your machine.
CutRanked timelines land in your project: primary selects, alternates, smart groups and the residual. Nothing is lost, everything is ranked.

The tour

Smart Select

Trims the dead weight inside every take and returns four ranked timelines. Your best moments, found for you.

Sound

Your music library, searchable like a streaming app. Tempo, key and mood are read from the audio itself, even tracks from years ago.

Asset Library

Every transition, preset, LUT, SFX and template in one search bar. Send an asset and it lands on your timeline and in the project folder at once.

Soundbites

Feed it a transcript and the strongest pulls come back grouped: hooks, quotes, emotional moments. Click a card and your playhead jumps there.

Inspiration

A reference library that remembers why you saved it. Links, files and notes, organised into collections.

Project Memory

Footage, music, references, exports and deliverables gathered per project, with a one click archive you can hand off or shelve.

A promise that matters

EditHive never moves, copies, renames or modifies your files. It builds an index that points back to where everything already lives. Analysis runs on your machine, and nothing about your footage is uploaded to train somebody else's model. Your folder structure stays exactly as you left it.
