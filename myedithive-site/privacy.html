Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Edithive ("we", "us") handles information across this website, the Edithive Smart Selects waitlist, and the Edithive Select desktop application and panels (the "Software").

The short version

The Software is local-first. Your footage, audio, projects, transcripts, and asset library stay on your own Mac. We do not upload, access, store, or analyse your media or your work. The Software has no advertising trackers and no usage analytics.

The desktop app — what stays on your device

Everything you work with lives locally on your computer:

  • your media files, projects, timelines, and exports;
  • transcripts and the analysis the engine produces;
  • your asset library and sound library indexes (these record where your files are on disk — the files themselves are never moved or copied off your machine);
  • your settings.

The analysis engine, transcription, and media tools all run on your Mac. None of this content is sent to us or to any third party.

The desktop app — the only things that leave your device

The Software makes a small number of outbound requests, and only these:

  • Licence activation & validation. To confirm your beta key or licence, the app contacts our licensing service and sends your licence key and a non-identifying device fingerprint (a hashed hardware identifier used to bind the licence to your machine and limit activations). It does not send your media or project content.
  • Update checks. The app may check our website for a newer version (a basic web request for a small version file). You decide whether to download an update; nothing installs automatically.
  • Features you explicitly trigger. Anything that needs the internet (e.g. downloading an asset you chose) only happens when you initiate it.

We do not collect telemetry, usage statistics, or behavioural data from the app.

The website & waitlist

When you submit the waitlist form, we collect what you provide — your name, email address, and optionally the type of editor you are. This site uses no advertising trackers and no third-party analytics; privacy-respecting request logs may be kept by our hosting provider, Netlify. Waitlist submissions are processed through Netlify Forms. We use these details only to contact you about the beta and launch, and we do not sell your information.

How we use information

Licence and waitlist information is used only to operate the Software and communicate with you about it. We do not sell your data, and we share it only with the service providers needed to run the licence service, the website, and email (e.g. our hosting and email providers).

Your choices

You can ask us to access or delete your waitlist or licence-account information at any time by contacting support@myedithive.com. Every email we send includes an option to opt out.

Children

The Software and this site are intended for professionals and are not directed to children under 16.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product develops. Material changes will be reflected by the date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@myedithive.com.
