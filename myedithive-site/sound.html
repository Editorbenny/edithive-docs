How it works

  1. 01

    Point it at your music folders

    Sound listens to every track and SFX locally. Tempo, key, energy and mood — read from the audio, not the filename.

    Styles that match how you think

    Detected styles like Cinematic or Ambient get you started. Then train your own — Wedding Energy, Nike Commercial, Hype Intro.

  3. 03

    Audition and drop, inside your editor

    Search how you think, hit play, send the winner to your timeline. Never leave Premiere.
